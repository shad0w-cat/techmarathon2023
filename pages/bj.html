Remember solving those grade school exercises where one had to rearrange jumbled words and sentences?
Remember the satisfaction of seeing everything finally click into place?

If you've started to feel nostalgic, then Tech Marathon '23 has the perfect event to satiate both your inner-child and geek -
'Bumble Jumble'.
The event will comprise of two rounds filled with jumbled, buggy incomplete code waiting for you to see the error of their ways...
Round 1 :
  • 50 questions including jumbled codes, functions with fill-ups, error-corrections, MCQ, etc. to be solved in maximum 30 minutes.
  • Top 3 competitors selected based on accuracy and response time progress into the Final Round.
Round 2(Final) :
  • 2 jumbled/incomplete code having error type questions to be solved in 60 minutes.
  • Language allowed: C++
  • Participants can compete on their own or in teams of two.
And here's what you can attain, besides, of course, the premium experience of reliving your carefree school days:
1st position: ₹3000
2nd position: ₹2000
3rd position: ₹1000
So don’t wait just go and register at the earliest!
Registration Deadline: 2nd February
