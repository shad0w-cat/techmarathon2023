"Everybody's a web designer until, you know, they actually try to do it."
Here is an opportunity for all the creative minds out there to let the coder artist inside you free. Show us your creativity and coding skills and compete with your counterparts in "WEB GATORS", organized by Sanganika- The Computer Science society of Deen Dayal Upadhaya College under the annual Tech Marathon '23.
If you are interested in web design and development then this competition is going to thrill you. Come on Codeshefs cook something to amaze each and every one of us and cherry on the cake!
Prelims (Round 1)
In the first round, we have 30 MCQs containing questions on Frontend, Internet Technologies and Backend.
We will judge you based on your speed and accuracy.
Main (Round 2)
In the second round i.e. development round, participants will be asked to develop a website based on the theme provided by us.
They have to present 40% of the prototype and it should be functional.

  • Team Size

  • Participants can only use the tech stacks mentioned.
  • All the registered participants have to be present on the event day.
  • If you are bringing your own laptop, make sure the softwares are pre-installed to avoid last-minute hassles.
  • The participants can only build the project on the given theme, no other project will be accepted.
